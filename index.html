About

I am a Syrian-German machine learning researcher focused on medical AI and multimodal biomedical data. My work spans model development, rigorous evaluation, open-source research software, and production deployment, with a particular focus on trustworthy AI — building systems that are reliable, reproducible, and suitable for real-world use.

I'm especially interested in multimodal models, empirical deep learning, and the engineering foundations of dependable, steerable AI systems. I'm currently pursuing a PhD at the Institute of Medical Informatics, RWTH Aachen University.

Trustworthy AI Multimodal learning Empirical deep learning Scalable ML systems Robustness & evaluation
